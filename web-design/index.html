Website questions, answered

How long does a typical website take?

A basic two page site can often be completed in one to two weeks once content is ready. Larger sites, rewrites, integrations, or automation work depend on scope and complexity.

What do I need before starting?

A clear goal, rough budget range, brand assets if you have them, and any existing website or system access. If you are not sure, the discovery call is where we sort that out.

Can you maintain the site after launch?

Yes. Monthly retainers start at $500 and can cover updates, monitoring, content changes, technical support, and ongoing improvements.

Do you build websites for businesses in my part of South Jersey?

Most likely yes. We work with businesses across Burlington, Camden, Ocean, and Mercer County, from Medford, Mount Holly, and Lumberton to Cherry Hill, Moorestown, Voorhees, Trenton, Ocean City, and Long Beach Island, plus remote clients. In person or fully remote both work.
