AI & AutomationCASE 01

Rebuilding a runaway AI classification pipeline

$285
final cost on a job first projected at $320,000

A document-classification system had become a budget problem. An earlier version quietly ran up tens of thousands in silent overages before anyone was even alerted. We rebuilt it from the ground up, and the whole story is in five numbers:

Cost, full jobprojected at $320,000→$285
Cost / documentover $1.00→a fraction of a cent
Speeda 2,500-document batch→~7 minutes (200,000 in ~9 hours)
What it readsentire 30-page contracts→only the text that carries the signal
Modelsone premium model for everything→right-sized models per task
Spend visibilitynone, until the bill came→real-time cost alerts

By hand, classifying 200,000 documents, some of them thirty-plus pages, would take one person years of full-time reading. The rebuilt pipeline clears them in about nine hours, for $285, on a job first projected at $320,000.
