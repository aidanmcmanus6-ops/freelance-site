Monitoring for Mount Laurel businesses

When your business runs on an app, an API, or an internal system, reliability is not a nice-to-have, it is the product. An outage or a slow degradation does not just annoy a customer; it breaks an SLA, stalls a partner, and erodes trust. The teams that stay ahead are the ones that see problems coming.

We set up real observability: uptime checks, performance dashboards, and alerting that reaches the right engineer fast, so issues are caught early instead of in a postmortem. For the mindset, see synthetic monitoring vs uptime.
