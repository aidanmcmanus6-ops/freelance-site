Monitoring questions, answered

What does monitoring actually catch?

Outages, slow response times, failed checkouts or contact forms, expired certificates, and broken pages, depending on what we set up. The goal is that you hear about a problem from us, not from a customer.

Do I need monitoring if my site seems fine?

Often yes. Most outages and silent failures happen without warning, and the cost is lost sales and trust. Monitoring is cheap insurance against the days your site is down and you do not know it.
