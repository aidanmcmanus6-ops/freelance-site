About MCM Integrated

A New Jersey studio for websites, automation, and uptime.

MCM Integrated is a two-person engineering studio based in Burlington County, New Jersey. Who we are

MCM Integrated was founded by Aidan Mcmanus and is run as a senior team of two. Between us we have built and supported software, secure cloud integrations, and large monitoring environments for mission-critical operations, including migrating 800+ devices between monitoring platforms and shipping secure Azure and Copilot Studio automation for enterprise teams.

We started MCM Integrated to give local businesses that same level of engineering without the cost and slow pace of a traditional agency. When you hire us, the people you talk to are the people writing the code. That means faster answers, clearer accountability, and work that is built to last rather than handed off and forgotten.

How we work

Every engagement starts with a short discovery call to understand the business goal before any code is written. We scope the smallest useful launch, build with regular visible progress, and ship cleanly with documentation and handoff notes. After launch, we can stay on through a monthly retainer for updates, automation improvements, and monitoring, or simply hand you a finished, well-documented project.

No outsourcing, no junior bait-and-switch, and no jargon for its own sake, just two senior engineers who own the outcome from first call to launch and beyond.
