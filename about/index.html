About MCM Integrated

Enterprise-grade engineering, at a small-business price.

MCM Integrated is a two-person engineering studio in Burlington County, New Jersey. We spent years building and running the websites, automation, and monitoring that large organizations depend on, and we started this studio to bring that same engineering to small and growing businesses, without the agency overhead, the junior hand-offs, or the enterprise invoice.

Why we started

Big companies have always had an unfair advantage online. They have senior engineers on staff, sites that load fast and almost never go down, automation quietly handling the busywork, and dashboards that catch problems before a single customer notices. Most small businesses get the opposite: a slow template site, no monitoring, an agency that disappears the day after launch, or a quote with an extra zero on the end.

We spent our careers on the enterprise side of that line, and the gap never sat right with us. A local shop, a contractor, a growing company — none of them should have to choose between amateur work and an enterprise budget. So we started MCM Integrated to close it: to give small businesses the same caliber of engineering the big players pay a fortune for, at a price that actually makes sense for a small business.

The way we keep it affordable is simple. There is no agency overhead, no account managers, and no junior staff learning on your project. The people who built enterprise systems are the same people who build yours, so you pay for the work, not the layers around it.

What that looks like in practice

This is not a pitch deck. It is the work. We rebuilt a runaway AI document pipeline that was projected to cost $320,000 and brought the job in at $285. We stood up the monitoring and alerting that backstopped a multi-million-dollar website launch through its riskiest window, catching issues before customers ever saw them. And we design and build full custom websites for around $1,500, the kind of site agencies routinely quote at five or six thousand.

How we work

Every engagement starts with a short discovery call to understand the business goal before any code is written. We scope the smallest useful launch, build with regular visible progress, and ship cleanly with documentation and handoff notes. After launch, we can stay on through a monthly retainer for updates, automation improvements, and monitoring, or simply hand you a finished, well-documented project.

No outsourcing, no junior bait-and-switch, and no jargon for its own sake, just two senior engineers who own the outcome from first call to launch and beyond.
